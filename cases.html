Background & Context

MedTech Regional Hospital Network is a mid-sized healthcare provider operating 12 hospitals and 85 outpatient clinics across three states in the southeastern United States...

Key Data Points & Market Context

  • Current State Performance: Patient satisfaction score of 71%; average patient wait time of 47 minutes; 28% administrative cost ratio
  • Technology Infrastructure: Five disparate EHR systems; 40% of clinics still paper-based; zero telehealth infrastructure
  • Financial Impact: Annual revenue $1.2B; $28M annual revenue leakage due to no-shows; $336M admin costs
  • Competitive Landscape: Two digital-native competitors captured 8% market share; 67% of patients aged 25-45 prefer digital options
  • Regulatory Environment: Interoperability mandated by 2026; $15-20M federal digital health funding; upcoming privacy regulations
  • Patient Demographics: 62% over 50, 25-45 fastest growing (18% yearly); 73% own smartphones; 45% interested in telehealth

Consulting Questions & Analytical Tasks

  1. Strategic Prioritization & ROI Analysis: Develop a prioritization framework for digital initiatives within $50M budget, calculate ROI, recommend phased implementation.
  2. Technology Integration & Change Management: Analyze options to unify or integrate EHR systems; design change management & training roadmap for 15,000 employees.
  3. Patient Portal & Telehealth Business Case: Build a business case for portal and telehealth; estimate impact on no-shows, acquisition, and visit costs; recommend pricing and quality strategy.
  4. Operational Efficiency Optimization: Identify top 3 processes to digitize/automate; quantify savings and efficiency gains; set Year 1/2 metrics.
  5. Competitive Response & Market Positioning: Develop strategy against digital-native competitors; propose marketing, positioning, and go-to-market plan for digital services.
