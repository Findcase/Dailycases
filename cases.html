Background & Context

MedTech Regional Hospital Network operates 12 hospitals and 85 outpatient clinics across three states. With 2,800 physicians and 15,000 employees serving over 450,000 patients annually, the network is recognized for clinical excellence. However, patient satisfaction dropped from 82% to 71% due to appointment delays, long wait times, and fragmented communication. Operational inefficiencies include 23-minute average check-in, 48-hour record retrieval delays, and administrative costs of 28% of total expenses.

Key Data Points & Market Context

  • Performance: Patient satisfaction 71%, wait time 47 mins, 23-min check-in, admin costs 28%
  • Technology: Five separate EHRs, 40% paper-based clinics, no telehealth, avg downtime 3.2h/month
  • Financial: Revenue $1.2B, $28M lost from no-shows, $336M admin costs
  • Competition: Two digital-native entrants with 8% market share; 67% patients 25-45 prefer digital booking
  • Regulations: Interoperability standards by 2026, $15–20M federal digital funding, upcoming privacy regulations
  • Demographics: 62% over 50, 25-45 fastest growing; 73% smartphone ownership; 45% interested in telehealth

Consulting Questions & Analytical Tasks

  1. Strategic Prioritization & ROI: Develop a framework to prioritize digital initiatives within $50M budget, calculate ROI, and recommend phased implementation.
  2. Technology & Change Management: Analyze unifying vs integrating EHRs; design change management & training for 15,000 employees.
  3. Patient Portal & Telehealth: Build business case; estimate impact on no-shows, patient acquisition, and facility costs; recommend pricing and quality strategy.
  4. Operational Efficiency: Identify top 3 processes to digitize; quantify savings; set Year 1/2 success metrics.
  5. Competitive Response: Develop strategy against digital-native competitors; propose marketing, positioning, and go-to-market plan.
